smart class

Method is same like normal class but we have to use information technology for teaching.In smart class, projector is used for presenting slides prepare by the faculty of that subject.They show slides on white screen.student have to listen and watch whatever teach by faculty.There is no need of note down whatever presented on the slide.faculty send this slide after class.
It is the modern way of teaching.
