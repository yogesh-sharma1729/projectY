normal class

In normal class,teacher took lectures on various subjects(subjects like physics,maths,english,chemistry). Teacher use chalk, duster , blackboard nowdays whiteboard and marker. Student note down in notebook whatever speek by teacher or written on board. Teacher give assignment and student have to complete it within time limit. Student have
to give exam on paper sheet and submit .after that, teacher evaluate answer sheet and marksheet is prepare according to the evaluation by teacher.
Student sit on benches in a clean and furnitured room. They have to carry a bag containing books and notebooks.
